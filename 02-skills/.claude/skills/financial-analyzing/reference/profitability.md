# Profitability Analysis Framework

## Key Profitability Metrics

- **Gross Profit**: Revenue - COGS
- **Gross Margin %**: Gross Profit ÷ Revenue × 100%
- **Operating Profit (EBIT)**: Revenue - COGS - Operating Expenses
- **Operating Margin %**: EBIT ÷ Revenue × 100%
- **Net Profit**: EBIT - Interest - Taxes
- **Net Margin %**: Net Profit ÷ Revenue × 100%

## Unit Economics

- **LTV** (Lifetime Value): ARPU × Average Customer Lifespan × Gross Margin
- **LTV:CAC**: Should be > 3:1 for healthy SaaS
- **Payback Period**: CAC ÷ (ARPU × Gross Margin)
- **Rule of 40**: Revenue Growth % + Profit Margin % should exceed 40%

## Profitability Stages

| Stage | Revenue Growth | Profit Margin | Strategy |
|-------|---------------|---------------|----------|
| Pre-PMF | N/A | Negative | Minimize burn |
| Growth | > 50% | -20% to 0% | Invest in growth |
| Scale | 20-50% | 0-15% | Balance growth & profit |
| Mature | < 20% | > 15% | Optimize efficiency |
