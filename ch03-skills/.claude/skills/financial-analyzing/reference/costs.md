# Cost Analysis Framework

## Key Cost Metrics

- **COGS** (Cost of Goods Sold): Direct costs of service delivery
- **Gross Margin**: (Revenue - COGS) ÷ Revenue × 100%
- **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost): Total Sales & Marketing ÷ New Customers
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: Customer Lifetime Value ÷ CAC (target: > 3:1)
- **Burn Rate**: Monthly cash outflow - Monthly cash inflow

## Cost Categories

1. **Infrastructure** — Cloud hosting, databases, CDN
2. **Personnel** — Salaries, benefits, contractors
3. **Sales & Marketing** — Advertising, events, tools
4. **R&D** — Engineering salaries allocated to new features
5. **G&A** — Office, legal, accounting, insurance

## Efficiency Benchmarks (SaaS)

| Metric | Good | Great |
|--------|------|-------|
| Gross Margin | > 70% | > 80% |
| CAC Payback | < 18 months | < 12 months |
| R&D % of Revenue | 15-25% | 20-30% |
| S&M % of Revenue | 20-40% | 15-25% |
