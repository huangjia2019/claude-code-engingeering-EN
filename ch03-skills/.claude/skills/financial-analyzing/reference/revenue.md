# Revenue Analysis Framework

## Key Revenue Metrics

- **MRR** (Monthly Recurring Revenue): Sum of all recurring subscription revenue
- **ARR** (Annual Recurring Revenue): MRR × 12
- **ARPU** (Average Revenue Per User): Total Revenue ÷ Active Users
- **Revenue Growth Rate**: (Current - Previous) ÷ Previous × 100%
- **Net Revenue Retention**: (Starting MRR + Expansion - Contraction - Churn) ÷ Starting MRR

## Revenue Categories

1. **Subscription Revenue** — Recurring monthly/annual fees
2. **Usage Revenue** — Pay-per-use charges (API calls, storage)
3. **One-time Revenue** — Setup fees, professional services
4. **Expansion Revenue** — Upgrades, add-ons from existing customers

## Red Flags

- Revenue growth < customer growth (ARPU declining)
- One-time revenue > 20% of total (unsustainable)
- Top 3 customers > 30% of revenue (concentration risk)
- Net retention < 100% (losing more than gaining from existing customers)
